Detailed Description

The basis for an analysis : a Run with its (optional) data . Contains S1S2Data for each of AM_BE_DATA , E_GAMMA_DATA , DM_DATA , AM_BE_CUT_DATA , E_GAMMA_CUT_DATA , DM_CUT_DATA . Contains S1S2Bands for the same data sets. S1S2Bands of (nominal) background are created; they are called: ER_BACKGROUND, ER_LEAKAGE, NR_BACKGROUND and ALL_BACKGROUNDS
